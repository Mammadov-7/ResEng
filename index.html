ResEng

Reservoir Engineering Tools · TU Clausthal

· ABOUT

ResEng is a web application designed to assist petroleum engineering students and researchers at TU Clausthal in performing reservoir permeability calculations. Built upon Darcy's Law and industry-standard models, the platform offers interactive tools for both single-phase and multi-phase flow analysis, supporting academic learning and applied research alike.

· ONE PHASE

Single-phase absolute permeability calculator based on Darcy's Law for linear and radial flow. Input known parameters — pressure drop, flow rate, fluid viscosity, and core geometry — and solve for any unknown variable. Ideal for laboratory core analysis, well testing, and introductory reservoir characterization.

· TWO PHASE

Two-phase relative permeability calculator for oil-water and gas-oil systems. Supports the Corey, Brooks-Corey, and LET models with adjustable saturation endpoints and exponents. Generates relative permeability curves used in reservoir simulation and recovery analysis.
